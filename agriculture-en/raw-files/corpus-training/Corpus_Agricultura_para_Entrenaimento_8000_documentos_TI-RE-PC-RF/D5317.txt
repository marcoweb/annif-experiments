#ITI#Mycoplasma gallisepticum-induced alterations in chicken red blood cells#FTI#
#IRE#Incubation of Mycoplasma gallisepticum with washed chicken red blood cells for 1 hr or 5 hr resulted in altered red blood cell surface morphology and perforations of the cells.#FRE#
