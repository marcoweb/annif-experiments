#ITI#The species composition and occurrence of thrips (Thysanoptera, Insecta) on winter rye in chosen regions of Poland#FTI#
#IRE#The work has determined the species composition of thrips and their occurrence of winter rye in the chosen soil-climatic regions of Poland. The occurrence of several species of thrips has been noted on winter rye, with Haplothrips aculeatus Fabricius as the dominant one. Limothrips cerealium Holiday has extended its occurrence in the western direction of Poland. Thrips on winter rye occurred most amply in the south-western region.#FRE#
