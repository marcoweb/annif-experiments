#ITI#Genetic diversity in Carthamus tinctorius (Asteraceae; safflower), An underutilized oilseed crop#FTI#
#IRE#Premise of the study: Underutilized crops are potentially valuable resources for meeting increasing food demands. Safflower, an oilseed crop, is an example of one such underutilized crop that thrives in moisture-limited areas. Characterization of the genetic diversity maintained within the gene pools of underutilized crops such as safflower is an important step in their further development.#FRE#
