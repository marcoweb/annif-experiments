#ITI#Morphometrics of Daucus (Apiaceae): A counterpart to a phylogenomic study#FTI#
#IRE#• Premise of study: Molecular phylogenetics of genome-scale data sets (phylogenomics) often produces phylogenetic trees with unprecedented resolution. A companion phylogenomics analysis of Daucus using 94 conserved nuclear orthologs supported many of the traditional species but showed unexpected results that require morphological analyses to help interpret them in a practical taxonomic context.#FRE#
