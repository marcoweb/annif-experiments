#ITI#Alnus subgenus alnus in the Eocene of western North America based on leaves, associated catkins, pollen, and fruits#FTI#
#IRE#• Premise of the study: The fossil record of alder (Alnus) is well known in the Cenozoic deposits throughout the northern hemisphere, based on numerous reports of the distinctive pollen, cone-like infructescences, staminate inflorescences, and leaves. However, our understanding of the systematic position of these fossils relative to the modern phylogeny of the genus has been limited because most fossils were described from only one organ.#FRE#
