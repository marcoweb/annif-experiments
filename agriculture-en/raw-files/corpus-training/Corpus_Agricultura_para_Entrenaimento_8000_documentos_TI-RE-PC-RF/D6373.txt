#ITI#Hughmillerites vancouverensis sp. nov. and the cretaceous diversification of Cupressaceae#FTI#
#IRE#Premise of the study: Two ovulate conifer cones, one of which is attached terminally to a short leafy shoot, reveal the presence of a new species of Hughmillerites in the Early Cretaceous Apple Bay flora of Vancouver Island, British Columbia, Canada. This ancient conifer expands the diversity of Cupressaceae in the Mesozoic and reveals details about the evolution of Subfamily: Cunninghamioideae.#FRE#
